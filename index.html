Our Story

Music As A
Movement

Radiant Groove Records was born in 2019 from a simple but radical conviction: music should be free — free to listen to, free to enter, and free from judgment. We are an independent electronic music label with a mission that goes beyond releasing tracks.

Today, we channel that founding energy into crafting bespoke electronic soundscapes for venues worldwide — while staying true to the ethos that got us here.

What We Do

Club Sound
Services

We create complete, venue-tailored electronic music experiences — from the opening ambient set to the peak-hour techno climax. Every soundtrack is designed to fit your crowd, your space, and your brand identity.

01

Night Club Soundtrack Packages

Full-night curated music programming. We compose and mix original electronic tracks tailored to your club's identity — tech house, techno, deep house, melodic — sequenced for maximum energy flow across your night.

Custom · Original · Exclusive
02

Beach Club & Daytime Vibes

Sunshine-soaked electronic soundscapes for beach clubs, pool parties, and luxury day venues. Afro house, organic house, balearic beats, and chillout electronica — music that feels as good as the weather.

Ambient · Tropical · Smooth
03

Bespoke Composition

Fully original tracks composed specifically for your brand — intro music, branded loops, stage intros, and exclusive releases only you own. Perfect for clubs wanting a truly unique sonic signature.

Exclusive Rights · White Label
04

Ongoing Soundtrack Subscription

Monthly fresh music drops for your venue. Stay ahead of trends with new original productions delivered to your inbox every month, fully mixed and ready to play.

Monthly · Fresh · Curated

Event & Festival Curation

Full musical direction for one-off events, festival stages, brand activations, and pop-up experiences. We handle the full sonic arc from start to finish — concept to closing track.

Events · Festivals · Brand Activations

Consultation & Sound Branding

Not sure what your venue sounds like yet? We work with you to define your sonic identity — genre, tempo, mood, transitions — and build a musical strategy that sets you apart from the competition.

Live Events

No Exceptions.

Since 2019, we've believed the dancefloor is a democratic space. Our live events have never had a ticket price. We don't do VIP queues, door policies based on appearance, or exclusive guest lists that shut people out.

Music unites. Our gigs are built around that principle. If you want to host a Radiant Groove Records event at your venue — or collaborate on a free community event — we'd love to make it happen.
